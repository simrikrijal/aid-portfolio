About

Crimson Tourism is the project based on the Assignment for 5th semester on The British College. It basically covers Admin entering the site and updating the destinations while other users are able to mark the destinations as their favorite.

Security Implementation

As security is crucial in any web application. I've implemented the feature of restricting the user to use password that contains at least 1 capital letter, a number and a symbol with at least 3 letters.

We Plan

About

We Plan is content management website that's written in core php. Actually, it was for clients who are residing in United States, for their event management business. Weplan is full fledged content management based system for dynamic static like website.

Color Combination

As client's requirement was crucially on reflecting their theme on the website, color combination was important factor on weplan. So I decided to go with their primary color (green) and secondary color (pink) theme for the overall website.

Two of the colors used to match the theme of the client are:
Primary Color: #2C8C7D
Secondary Color: #D267A0

Features

Client also demanded that they should be able to upload small snippet of video, although they upload the short clip of their choice but they are restricted to upload upto 7MB of size only.
